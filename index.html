David Hover - Chiropractor

Welcome

Cheltenham Chiropractor David Hover is based at Move Chiropractic on Rodney Road, Cheltenham.

Experienced in sports/fitness therapies as well as general/work related conditions, David specialises in the diagnosis and treatment of neck and back disorders, along with other conditions of the muscles and joints in the rest of the body.

Treatments Provided

As a fully qualified Chiropractor, David is skilled in Spinal Manipulation Therapy and Joint Mobilisation, combined with a strong focus on soft tissue/muscle techniques such as Myofascial Release, Trigger Point Therapy, Kinesiology Taping, PIR/PNF, and Deep Tissue Massage.

As a part of his manual treatment sessions, additional exercise/rehabilitation advice can be invaluable. With a particular interest in weight training, powerlifting and yoga, David is experienced in a wide range of effective strategies for the office, gym and sporting environment alike.
